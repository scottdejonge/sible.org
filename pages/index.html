A platform that
puts people first.

Sible empowers people to share and access detailed accessibility information. From elevation maps to ramp locations, hazard information to trip planners, Sible improves places by making them easier to navigate – for everyone.

Features Register

Better access
benefits everyone.

1 in 5 Australians have some form of disability. That’s over 4 million people. For many, something as “simple” as crossing a street, going to the shops or finding a toilet can be incredibly challenging.

Sible helps people overcome ongoing obstacles and makes it easier to get around. Using crowd-sourced data, it connects people to all the information they need to help plan their day.

About Register

Empowering
People

Sible will provide people with accessibility information for locations, businesses, services and amenities. This will cater diverse needs:

  • Visual
  • Auditory
  • Motor
  • Cognitive

Enhancing
Places

Sible will enable business to provide detailed accessibility information for their locations. This includes details about:

  • Parking & Transport
  • Ramps & Landings
  • Entrances & Exits
  • Services & Amenities

Evolving
Policy

Sible will inform policy that better suits the requirements of people living with accessibility needs, and inform the way we design and plan our cities.

  • City Planning
  • Transportation
  • Support Services
  • Public Amenities
