Better access

Sible is a digital platform that provides detailed accessibility information, improving the places in which we live by connecting more residents to services and empowering them to navigate with ease.

Features

Empowering
People

Sible will provide people with accessibility information for locations, businesses, services and amenities. This will cater diverse needs:

  • Visual
  • Auditory
  • Motor
  • Cognitive

Enhancing
Places

Sible will enable business to provide detailed accessibility information for their locations. This includes details about:

  • Parking & Transport
  • Ramps & Landings
  • Entrances & Exits
  • Services & Ammenities

Evolving
Policy

Sible will allow government to create policy that better suits the requirements of people living with accessibility needs, and inform the way we design and plan our cities.

  • BLAH
