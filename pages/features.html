Information
for every place.

Places are assessed against a range of criteria that review accessibility from all perspectives.

Reviews

Access and provide feedback on locations, facilities and amenities.

Ratings

Assign a rating to locations, facilities and amenities, and access other ratings.

Media

Upload or view media (such as a photos) that provides context to accessibility.

Hazards

Find the most efficient routes by avoiding hazards and physical barriers.

Elevation

Avoid steep gradients with elevation maps and optimised routes.

Discovery

Find recommendations for places that suit your requirements.

Search

Search locations, businesses and amenities and filter by requirements.

Transportation

Organise your day with transport options that suit your needs and journey.
